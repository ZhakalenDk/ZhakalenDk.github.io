<----------About Us---------->

Who we are

We're a small Indie Group of 4 people located in Southern Denmark.
We're Gamers ourselves!
We're intrigued by good storyline, beautiful visuals and stunning Original Gameplay as much as you are.
Despite the fact that we're still learning we do have a lot of knowledge and we always find a solution to every problem,
which means we're building our boat while we're sailing it.

How did we get here?

We always wanted to make a game and one day at a Party we ended up talking about it.
We quickly realised that this could actually become reality, so we began working on it. We could fill 3 roles, which was where we began.

* Writer
* Level Designer
* Programmer

We sat down, brainstormed and ended up here
About to make our first game! This is so exciting!
